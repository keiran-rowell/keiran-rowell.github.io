Codon Rarity 3D Viewer

Visualise co-translational folding bottlenecks in protein structures

How it works: Upload a PDB structure and sequence, select an organism to see codon rarity mapped onto the 3D protein structure. Rare codons (red) may indicate co-translational folding pauses.

Codon Rarity Scale

Rare (slow translation) Common (fast translation)

Codon Optimisation & Harmonisation

Two Approaches:
Optimisation: Replace codons with the most common synonymous codons in the expression organism (maximises expression).
Harmonisation: Match codon rarity patterns between source and expression organisms (preserves co-translational folding).
